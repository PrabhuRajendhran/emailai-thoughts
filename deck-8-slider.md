# Q&A Defense Sheet – Likely Tough Questions

Below are the questions you’re most likely to get from MD, CFO, Risk, or CTO.

---

## 1. What if the AI misroutes critical regulatory emails?

**Answer:**

We do not allow blind automation.

Routing is confidence-based:

* High-confidence → auto-route
* Medium-confidence → optional review
* Low-confidence → mandatory human escalation

Each business unit can set its own risk thresholds.

Additionally:

* Full audit logs
* Decision traceability
* Continuous drift monitoring

This is controlled automation, not autonomous AI.

---

## 2. Why not just hire more people instead of investing in AI?

**Answer:**

Hiring scales linearly with volume.
This platform scales exponentially.

If volume grows 15%, headcount must grow 15%.
AI infrastructure handles growth with marginal incremental cost.

Over time, automation becomes cheaper and more consistent than manual routing.

---

## 3. What is the implementation risk?

**Answer:**

Low to moderate, managed in phases:

* Start with limited LOB rollout
* Measure accuracy
* Tune thresholds
* Expand gradually

No “big bang” deployment.

Value is delivered incrementally within 12 weeks.

---

## 4. What happens if categories change?

**Answer:**

The architecture is modular.

Adding or modifying categories does not require rebuilding the system.

We update:

* Category definitions
* Example data
* Thresholds

Only targeted tuning is needed — not full retraining.

---

## 5. How do we ensure explainability?

**Answer:**

Each routing decision logs:

* Matched signals
* Similar historical examples
* Confidence score

We can explain why a decision was made.

This is critical for regulatory environments.

---

## 6. What is the financial upside?

**Answer (Conservative Framing):**

Even modest automation reduces thousands of daily manual hours.

Additionally:

* Faster response times
* Lower SLA breach penalties
* Reduced compliance risk exposure

The ROI improves as volume grows.

---

## 7. Could this become a black-box dependency?

**Answer:**

No.

* Modular components
* Version-controlled models
* Human override capability
* Rollback mechanism

We maintain operational control at all times.

---

## 8. How scalable is this beyond email?

**Answer:**

Highly scalable.

The same platform can process:

* Client documents
* Regulatory filings
* Case management intake
* Chat & ticketing systems

This becomes enterprise AI routing infrastructure — not an email tool.

---

## 9. Why now?

**Answer:**

Because volume and complexity have already crossed the threshold where manual routing is efficient.

Delaying increases cost and operational risk.

Implementing now positions us ahead of volume growth — not reacting to it.
